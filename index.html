Her Journey

Pinky's spiritual journey began in the sacred city of Rishikesh, where she immersed herself in the ancient wisdom of yoga. After completing her advanced teacher training, she dedicated herself to sharing the transformative power of yoga with students worldwide. Her recent completion of a 10-day Vipassana meditation retreat has deepened her understanding of mindfulness and inner peace.

"My mission is to help every individual discover their inner strength, find peace in chaos, and achieve holistic wellness through authentic yoga practice. I believe yoga is not just physical exercise but a way of life that transforms the body, mind, and soul."
